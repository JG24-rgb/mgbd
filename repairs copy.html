Repair Process

1

Contact Us

Reach out via phone, email, or our online booking form to schedule an appointment. Provide details about your vehicle and the issues you are experiencing.

2

Receive Confirmation

Our team will confirm your appointment time and date, and provide any necessary pre-appointment instructions. We’ll also offer a preliminary estimate based on the information provided.

3

Drop Off Your Vehicle

Bring your vehicle to our repair center at the scheduled time. We offer a comfortable waiting area if you prefer to wait, or you can arrange for a courtesy shuttle or loaner vehicle.

4

Inspection & Estimate

Our certified technicians will conduct a thorough inspection of your vehicle. We will then provide a detailed diagnostic report and a final estimate for the required repairs.

5

Approval & Repair

Once you approve the estimate, our team will begin the repair work. We will keep you informed throughout the process and notify you when your vehicle is ready for pickup. After final checks, you can collect your vehicle and drive away with confidence.
